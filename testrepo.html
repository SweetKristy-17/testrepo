Inspirational Thoughts About Kindness

From stoicism to Buddhism to Christianity, all the world’s great religions and philosophies have emphasized the importance of kindness. These thoughts, by some of the greatest teachers of compassion and kindness in the world, can inspire us to extend that attitude to our work and personal lives. “Kindness in words creates confidence. Kindness in thinking creates profoundness. Kindness in giving creates love.”
